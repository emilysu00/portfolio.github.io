Team：Chen Yixun, Ni Minjia, Du Guanyi, Liang Yinxuan, Peng Zhimin, Su Yenhua, Su Xinya, Wei Shiyun, Xie Xinci, Li Chenyu, Zhang Xuan, Liao Zhongyi

Through the implementation of CIS and IP design, we aim to add interest to the Tan Hua Fu in Xiong jiang Town, promoting its culture and enhancing the value. Our goal is to contribute to the cultural revitalization of the town by infusing creativity into its identity, fostering a sense of intrigue, and ultimately increasing its overall cultural and economic significance.

IP character design

Once upon a time, there was a small village nestled at the foot of a mountain, situated closely along the banks of the Min River. In a certain year, heavy rainfall upstream caused a deluge significant enough to submerge an entire mountain. As a result, the downstream river surged, and the small village was engulfed, leaving no survivors. Centuries later, people once again built a village on the riverbank, halfway up the mountain. In this village, there was a guardian deity, said to be a former villager from the submerged settlement. Several centuries after the village had been submerged, he emerged from the waters, accompanied by a small dog and a bat. Only during village celebrations would he quietly blend into the crowd, bringing with him the tales of the submerged village.
