LongChen Paper
CIS design

Team：Su Yenhua, Li Chenyu, Li Shuwei, Zhang Zhilan, Lin Pinyun, Peng Zhimin, Lin Jieru

To design a CIS system for LongChen Paper Industry, facilitating future design applications. Emphasis is placed on highlighting LongChen's efficient utilization of resource recycling and regeneration technologies. The design incorporates the principles of recycling, circular economy, and carbon reduction, aligning with the core business of producing industrial paper. The design direction is centered around the keywords "Environmental Protection, Circular Economy, Paper Material, and Industry."
