Wave Font design

By Su Yenhua

Integrating by the flowing essence of waves, this font aims to capture the lively and surging nature of the sea. Based on a rounded style, the font takes on a more flattened and wide shape, emphasizing horizontal variations. At the end of each stroke and curve, splashing water patterns are incorporated to infuse the font with the dynamic energy reminiscent of ocean waves.
