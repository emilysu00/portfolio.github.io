Bubble Monster
Soap Design

（ongoing）

Team：Su Yenhua, Chen Yujie, Huang Youzhen

Focused on designing soap characters, the team developed elements such as logo, logotype, packaging, character animations, and web design. The goal is to attract younger with adorable soap character designs, to reduce wastes by shower gel and shampoo bottles.
soap design
packaging design
dynamic design
In a web page, provide a comprehensive introduction to the characters in our monster-themed setting, with their characteristics related to pollution. Engage the audience by presenting the origins, traits, and dangers of these pollutants in an entertaining manner. Additionally, incorporate our design motivation to convey the severity of environmental harm caused by bath products such as shower gel and shampoo. Communicate our aspirations for achieving specific goals through this design.
